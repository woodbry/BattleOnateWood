Reimbursement Calculator

Each employee is allowed to claim up to $1000 in tuition reimbursement a year. The amount available to an employee is reset on the new year. Event types have different standard reimbursement coverage: University Courses 80%, Seminars 60%, Certification Preparation Classes 75%, Certification 100%, Technical Training 90%, Other 30%. After a Benefits Coordinator has approved a reimbursement, the reimbursement is pending until a passing grade or presentation over the event is provided.
